~ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ~

How far in advance should I book my session?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for portrait sessions and 6-12 months in advance for weddings to ensure availability.

What should I wear for my photo session?

We provide a detailed style guide after booking that includes recommendations for colors, patterns, and styles that photograph well. Generally, solid colors and avoiding busy patterns works best.

How long until I receive my photos?

Our standard turnaround time is 2-3 weeks for portrait sessions and 4-6 weeks for weddings. Rush processing is available for an additional fee.

Do you offer prints and albums?

Yes! We offer professional prints, canvases, and custom-designed albums. These can be ordered through your private online gallery after your session.
